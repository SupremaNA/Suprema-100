THIS DEALER Program (“Program”) is made as of the date of enrollment by and between Suprema America LLC (SA), 250 International Parkway, Lake Mary, Florida Suite 134, 32746 and (found in the enrollment documentation) (“Dealer”), with respect to the following:

A.  The objective of the Dealer Program is to (i) foster good market penetration for SA Products throughout North America by selecting companies to be “Authorized SA 100 Dealers” which can in the aggregate adequately service all geographic markets throughout North America, (ii) to enhance the ability of SA Products to compete with the products of other manufacturers by requiring Authorized SA Dealers to install and service the SA Products which they sell with the goal of encouraging end user acceptance of SA Products, (iii) to provide training to Authorized SA Dealers’ personnel and to set standards for service of SA Products to enhance end-user satisfaction with SA Products, and (iv) to limit the number of Authorized SA Dealers in order to encourage such dealers to devote sufficient financial and personnel resources to the sale, installation and servicing of SA Products.

B.  Dealer wishes to become an authorized dealer for SA products and SA is willing to appoint Dealer an authorized Suprema 100 dealer of SA products, all on the terms and conditions set forth below.

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

1.Appointment of Dealer as an Authorized SA Dealer.

SA hereby appoints Dealer as an Authorized Suprema 100 Dealer and Dealer hereby accepts such appointment.

2.Term of the Program.

The term of this Program (the “Term”) shall commence as of the date of enrollment and shall continue until terminated pursuant to Section 4 below.

3.Purchase of SA Products.

During the Term, Dealer will purchase SA Products directly from Suprema Authorized Distribution Partners.

During the Term, Dealer will use its best efforts to maximize its sales of SA Products. All sales by Dealer of SA Products must be to the End Users.

4.Termination of Program.

The Dealer 100 Program shall be terminated or adjusted under the following circumstances:

4.1.Upon mutual agreement of the parties hereto.
4.2.If Dealer does not meet the Annual Sales Volume (see Table 1 above) they may be adjusted to the appropriate Tier of the Program.
5.Other Authorized Dealers

Dealer acknowledges and agrees that SA retains the right in its sole and absolute discretion (i) to determine the total number of other Authorized SA Dealers, (ii) to designate whomever it wishes to be an Authorized SA Dealer, and (iii) to establish the same or different terms and conditions for others to become and remain Authorized SA Dealers.

6.Definitions.

As used in this document for following terms shall have the following meanings:

6.1.“Authorized SA Dealer” shall mean a Person who is authorized to sell and distribute SA Products.
6.2.“End-Users” shall mean Persons who acquire SA Products for installation into their facilities and residences.
6.3.“Person” shall mean any individual, corporation, limited liability company, partnership, association, trust, or other entity.
6.4.“Qualification Standards” shall mean all industry certifications requirements, SA sponsored training programs requirements, and/or technical exam minimum scores requirements set by STA, which requirements may be changed or modified from time to time in SA’s sole and absolute discretion.
6.5.“Qualified Systems Designers” and “Qualified Technicians” shall mean those system designers and technicians who have met and continue to meet the Qualification Standards.
6.6.“Industry Norms” shall mean the response times and service levels provided by other video security Dealers in similar geographic areas as established by surveys of end users.
6.7.“STW Products” shall mean all [close circuit television] products manufactured by or for SA and which are distributed in North America. The current list of SA Products are attached hereto as Schedule A. Dealer acknowledges and agrees that STW in its sole and absolute discretion may from time to time (i) modify the list of SA Products by adding to or deleting from said list specific products and/or (ii) change or modify any of the SA Products.
6.8.“Technical Support” shall mean technical advice provided to end users by telephone or if necessary, by field visits as necessary to enable end users to use SA products properly and effectively.
7.Notices.

All notices and other communications given or made pursuant hereto shall be in writing and shall be deemed to have been duly given or made (i) as of the date delivered, if delivered personally or if sent by facsimile or e-mail, provided that the facsimile or e-mail is promptly confirmed by written confirmation sent by registered or certified U.S. mail (postage prepaid, return receipt requested), or (ii) three (3) days after being mailed, if mailed by registered or certified U.S. mail (postage prepaid, return receipt requested) to the parties hereto at the addresses set forth in the enrollment documentation (or at such other address for a party as shall be specified by like notice, except that notices of changes of address shall be effective upon receipt).

8.Governing Law; Jurisdiction.

This Program shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Texas without giving effect to that State’s choice of law rules.

9.Dispute Resolution.

Arbitration. Any dispute, claim or controversy arising out of or relating to this Program or the breach, termination, enforcement, interpretation or validity thereof, including the determination of the scope or applicability of this Program to arbitrate, shall be determined by arbitration in Lake Mary, Florida, before a single independent arbitrator. The arbitration shall be administered by JAMS (www.jamsadr.com) pursuant to its Comprehensive Arbitration Rules and Procedures. Judgment on the Award may be entered in any court having jurisdiction. This clause shall not preclude parties from seeking provisional remedies in aid of arbitration from a court of appropriate jurisdiction.

10.Successors in Interest.

This Program Contract may not be assigned or transferred by any party hereto without the prior written consent of all other parties hereto, except in connection with the sale of all or substantially all of the assets and properties of SA or the merger, or other reorganization of SA. Except as otherwise provided herein, all provisions of this program contract shall be binding upon, inure to the benefit of, and be enforceable by and against the respective successors and permitted assigns of any of the parties to this program contract.
